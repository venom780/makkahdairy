WHY MAKKAH DAIRY ?


At MAKKAH DAIRY AND MILKSHOP, we only provide Raw Farm Fresh milk and you can be sure every pack has come from our own herd of dairy cows. Our dairy cows are fed, housed managed to the highest standards.  It takes a bit more time and a lot more work but it’s the only way to guarantee quality from the cow to the consumer. At MAKKAH DAIRY AND MILKSHOP we believe if it’s worth doing, it’s worth doing right!

That way our customers can be sure the milk entering our packing process has 100% traceability. But it doesn’t stop there; we are just not about producing packing 100% certifiable milk but also the supply chain itself. We work with our premium retailers to ensure that when you choose a MAKKAH DAIRY product, it is as fresh nutritious as nature intended. It’s our commitment to quality that ensures MAKKAH DAIRY tastes the way milk should – good and fresh!

From the food that our cows eat and the comfort they enjoy to our milking process packing, we do it all at our integrated dairy so we can ensure the highest quality as per international standards. From our farm to your fridge, MAKKAH DAIRY needs a wee bit more attention because we are additive free and don’t add anything to improve the quality, taste, or texture of our milk so the milk doesnot last longer
